## Why LearnKit

LearnKit helps you study what you write, without leaving your vault.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Companion Workflow" icon="seti:chat">
    Ask questions about notes, review notes with feedback, generate tests, and create flashcards from the same context.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Markdown-First Flashcards" icon="seti:markdown">
    Author cards directly in your notes so your study material stays close to the source and easy to maintain.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rich Card Types" icon="seti:library">
    Use basic and reversed cards, cloze deletions, image occlusion, multiple choice, and ordered sequences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="FSRS Scheduling" icon="seti:calendar">
    FSRS spaces cards intelligently so you see material when it matters instead of reviewing on fixed intervals.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
